About Our Department

The Computer Science & Engineering Department is established in 2013 at the University Of Barishal.It is committed to excellence in education, research and innovation in the field of computer science and technology.

We offer comprehensive undergraduate and graduate programs designed to prepare students for successful careers. Our faculties are influencing students in many sectors of computer science like Competative Programming, Data Science, Web and App develpoment, Machine learning etc.

  • Experienced faculty and research members
  • Industry-relevant curriculum and higher lab facilities for better learning
  • Internship opportunities
  • Active research technologies
