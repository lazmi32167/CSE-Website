Admission Requirements

Steps to start your journey with us

Bachelor's Program
  • Higher secondary certificate or equivalent
  • Selected in GST admission test
  • Mathematics proficiency
  • English proficiency (international students)
  • Personal statement
Master's Program
  • Bachelor's degree in CS or related field
  • Minimum 3.0 GPA
  • TOEFL/IELTS (international students)
  • 3 letters of recommendation
  • Statement of purpose
