Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

Admission requirements vary by program level. For undergraduate programs, we require a high school diploma, standardized GST admission test scores, and strong mathematics background. Graduate programs require a bachelor's degree in CS or related field and letters of recommendation.

Campus visits can be scheduled through our admissions office. Contact us at admissions@cse.bu.edu or call +1 (555) 123-4570 to arrange a personalized tour of our facilities and meet with faculty members.

We offer numerous research opportunities for motivated undergraduates, including paid research assistant positions, independent study courses and summer research programs. Students can work on projects in AI, cybersecurity, data science and other areas.

Our career services include resume review, interview preparation, job search assistance, and networking events with industry professionals. We maintain strong relationships with top tech companies and have a 95% job placement rate for graduates.
