Our History

More than one decade of excellence in computer science education

2013 - Department Established

Established as one of the first computer science departments in the region with 5 faculty members and 50 students.

2018 - First batch graduated

Introduced Master's programs, establishing our reputation for advanced research and graduate education.

2022 - First Googler from CSE department

Abu Saem Shefatullah,a student from CSE 3rd batch got his joob at Google which we consider as a great achievement for the whole department.

2022 - Mobile Computing Lab Opended

A MC lab was established in the dept for the better researche and programming practise for students.

2024 - All the labs and classroom was completly digitalized and Air-Conditioned.
